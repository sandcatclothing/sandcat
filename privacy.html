Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

1. Who we are

SANDCAT (“we”, “us”, “our”) operates the website sandcat.es. You can contact us at contacto@sandcat.es.

2. What data we collect

  • Newsletter: email address.
  • Orders: name, email, shipping address, postal code, ordered items, payment method (no card data stored by us), total, timestamps.
  • Technical: basic logs for security and service operation.

3. How we use your data (purposes)

  • Newsletter: to send you updates and marketing communications (with your consent).
  • Orders: to process and fulfil your purchases, send confirmations, manage support and returns (contract performance).
  • Security/operation: to maintain and protect our services (legitimate interest).

4. Legal bases

  • Consent: newsletter and optional marketing.
  • Contract: order processing and service delivery.
  • Legitimate interest: security, fraud prevention and service reliability.

5. Data sharing and processors

We use trusted providers to operate our services, including:

  • Google (Google Sheets / Apps Script) to store orders and newsletter subscriptions.
  • Hosting/CDN (e.g., Vercel or similar) to deliver the website securely.
  • Email services to send order confirmations and transactional emails.
  • Payment gateway (if enabled later, e.g., PayPal) to process payments. We do not store card details.
These providers process data only under our instructions to provide the service.

6. International transfers

Depending on your location and our providers, data may be processed in the EU and/or other countries. We rely on appropriate safeguards where required by law.

7. Data retention

  • Newsletter: until you unsubscribe or withdraw consent.
  • Orders: for the period necessary to fulfil legal, tax and accounting obligations.

8. Your rights

You may have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict or object to processing, and data portability, in accordance with applicable law. To exercise your rights or withdraw consent, contact contacto@sandcat.es.

9. Children

Our services are not directed at children. If you believe we collected data from a minor, please contact us.

10. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the latest version on this page with the “Last updated” date.
